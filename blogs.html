Top 5 Techniques to Boost Productivity

Time management is a skill that can be learned. Start by prioritising tasks, using short focused sessions and scheduling breaks. Use a weekly planner to visualise commitments and delegate when possible. Consider digital tools such as Trello or Todoist to organise tasks and set reminders. Making small consistent changes to routines can compound into significant improvements in productivity and reduce stress over time.

Building Habits That Stick

Small wins are the foundation of long-term change. Establish micro-habits, track progress and celebrate milestones. Habit stacking — attaching a new habit to an existing routine — increases the chance of success. Use habit trackers and accountability partners to maintain momentum. Reflect weekly to adjust goals and keep them aligned with your priorities.
